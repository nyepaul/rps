# Understanding Your Pension Inputs

The Retirement Planning System is built for flexibility. While we have clarified that your primary pension is **$120,000 / year total**, you can adjust this value at any time to explore "What If" scenarios.

## Where to Change Values

### Annual Pension
To change your yearly pension income:
1. Open the **Profile & Data** tab.
2. Scroll to **Income Streams & Pensions**.
3. Edit the existing "Employer Pension" entry.
4. Adjust the **Amount**, **Start Date**, or **Survivor %**.

### Lump Sum Pension
To see the impact of a one-time payout instead of annual income:
1. Delete the "Employer Pension" from **Income Streams**.
2. Go to the **Assets & Investments** table.
3. Add a new row:
   - **Name**: Pension Lump Sum
   - **Account Type**: Pension (Lump Sum)
   - **Value**: [Enter your payout amount]
